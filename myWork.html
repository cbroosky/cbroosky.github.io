Automated Hot Cocoa Maker

I have to get up at 5:30 every morning and I enjoy a cup of cocoa, I hate making it though. By the time I am ready to go, I don't have any time left to make hot chocolate. So I had the idea to make an automated hot chocolate maker. The idea is that I can press a button the night before telling it I want cocoa in the morning. It will then, at a set time fire up and make a nice pot of cocoa. I haven't had too much time to work on the code, but it will also interface through my home automation server.
